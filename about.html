About

Hi, I am Jack.

I work across human-centered design, engineering, animation, and film to build experiences that are thoughtful, technically grounded, and visually expressive.

I am studying Human Centered Design & Engineering at the University of Washington, with a minor in Digital Experimental Arts. My work often moves between research, interface design, prototyping, 3D production, and storytelling.

I have hands-on experience with Blender, Unreal Engine, Autodesk Inventor, product prototyping, user research, and visual communication. I am especially interested in projects where technical systems and creative direction need to support one another.

I am based in Seattle and plan to move to New York City after graduation.

I am open to design, creative technology, animation, and product roles, as well as collaborative projects that sit between disciplines.
