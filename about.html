About

Welcome. I'm Jack.

I try to live at the intersection of engineering, art, design and technology — work that captures who we are, how we got here, and how we express both.

I'm a Human Centered Design & Engineering student at the University of Washington, with a minor in Digital Experimental Arts.

I've always had a passion for animation — a medium that combines my adjacent interests into one. I have professional technical experience with Blender, Unreal Engine, and Autodesk Inventor to name a few, but my skillset extends far beyond naming software.

I live in Seattle, but I'm planning to move to NYC after graduation.

Please reach out — whether with work inquiries or just to network.
