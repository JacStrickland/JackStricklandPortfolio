Visual Communication / Mar 7, 2025

RIOT Film Festival

A 10-week visual identity system for a University of Washington animation and mixed-media festival, spanning posters, signage, wayfinding, and a mobile ticketing prototype.

ClientHCDE 308
RolePM, Researcher, UI/UX Designer
FocusBrand system, wayfinding, mobile prototype

Background

Core value proposition

RIOT Film Festival was positioned as a platform for emerging artists to experiment, challenge conventional storytelling, and present work in an environment that feels as original as the films themselves.

Unique selling point

Rather than follow the polished conventions of traditional festival branding, the identity embraces controlled disorder through bold typography, layered media, sketch-like marks, and a triadic color palette.

Results

The final system translated across print and digital touchpoints and was presented to professors and peers. The direction reinforced RIOT as a festival built around experimentation, creative freedom, and student-made work with a distinct point of view.
